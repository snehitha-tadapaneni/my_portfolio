Environmental Insights from the NYC Street Tree Census & Air Quality Data

Tech: Tableau | Duration: Mar – May 2025

  • Conducted exploratory data analysis on NYC’s Street Tree Census and Community Air Survey datasets to identify spatial patterns in tree health, biodiversity, and air quality.
  • Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard visualizing tree density, species diversity, pollution levels, and stewardship impact across 59 community districts.
  • Performed regression analysis revealing a statistically significant negative correlation (R² = 0.557, p < 0.0001) between tree species diversity and PM2.5 air pollution.
  • Mapped tree health by stewardship levels, highlighting how local involvement improves urban forest vitality. Integrated geospatial data using latitude & longitude joins and community district shapefiles to align tree and pollution datasets.
  • Derived actionable insights to support urban sustainability planning, community engagement, and environmental equity.
